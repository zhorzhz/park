Unlimited access to the Park

Enjoy the Park from morning until night for every day of your stay. You will never get bored in our park and with every visit you will find new attractions full which are unique in the world.

Free parking

Take the advantage of free parking right by your accommodation and the Park entrances. Our Parking place is enough for every visitor. If you are comming by bike, no problem we have a parking for bikes as well. Our parking is also equipped with chargins stations for electric vehicles, so while you will be enjoying our park your car will get fully charged and ready for you.

Covid measures

We care about health and safety of our visitors. Our attractions are being cleanes and desinfected several times a day. You will need to Wear a mask during the whole period of in our park. To make sure that you are well informed about COVID-19 prevention measures, please check our warning section. If you are traveling from other countries please make sure to check the information about international risk areas and follow the measures.

You are not a big movie fan, but still want to discover our park?

No worries our park is designed for everyone, so you will enjoy our park even if you are not a big movie fan. Check our interactive show section to get to know current most loved attraction movies.
